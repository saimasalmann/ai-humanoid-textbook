# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook. This comprehensive educational resource covers the complete pipeline from perception to interaction in embodied artificial intelligence systems.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for university students and developers interested in creating intelligent, physical AI systems. It covers:

- ROS 2 fundamentals as the robotic nervous system
- Gazebo simulation for safe development and testing
- NVIDIA Isaac platform for AI-powered robotics
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems for embodied intelligence
- Complete capstone project with an autonomous conversational humanoid robot

## Learning Objectives

After completing this textbook, you will be able to:

1. Design and implement ROS 2 nodes for robotic systems
2. Create and simulate humanoid robots in Gazebo
3. Integrate AI perception and decision-making systems
4. Build Vision-Language-Action pipelines for robotic interaction
5. Implement a complete autonomous humanoid robot system

## Course Structure

This textbook follows a 13-week university course structure:

- **Weeks 1-2**: Physical AI foundations
- **Weeks 3-5**: ROS 2 development
- **Weeks 6-7**: Gazebo simulation
- **Weeks 8-10**: Isaac platform and AI integration
- **Weeks 11-12**: Humanoid kinematics and control
- **Week 13**: Conversational robotics capstone

## Safety Disclaimer

When working with physical robotic systems, always follow proper safety protocols. Ensure proper supervision during all testing phases and maintain emergency stop capabilities. This textbook emphasizes safety-first development practices throughout all modules.
